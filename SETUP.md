# HirenextAI Platform Setup

## Install HirenextAI Extension

1. Open Chrome
2. Go to: `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable "Developer mode" (top right)
4. Click "Load unpacked"
5. Select the `extension/` folder located at `c:\Users\mindc\Downloads\hirenextai new thing with antigravaty\extension`
6. The extension will appear in your Chrome toolbar.

## How to use:

1. Login to HirenextAI (`localhost:3000` or production URL)
2. Find a job in chat
3. Click the "Apply with AI" button
4. Extension automatically opens the job application page (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
5. AI fills the form automatically
6. A white glow appears on the browser edges while the AI is working
7. Review the filled form and screenshot
8. Click "Submit Application" in the bottom right corner
9. Done! Check your Applications tab in the dashboard

## Stop AI anytime:
Click the red "Stop AI" button in the bottom bar to cancel the process at any point.

## Important Notes:
- Extension connects to backend via JWT
- User profile fetched from backend
- Cover letter generated by AI
- Screenshots sent to HirenextAI chat
- Application auto-added to tracker
- Works on: LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Glassdoor, Internshala
- Does NOT auto-submit (user must manually review and submit)
- AI can be stopped anytime
